Maze Bound — Support

Need help, found a bug, or have feedback? You’re in the right place.

Maze Bound is a minimalist grid-based arcade game. If something doesn’t work as expected (crashes, freezing, weird collisions, missing score updates, etc.), you can reach out and I’ll take a look.

Quick troubleshooting

  • Force close the app and reopen it.
  • Make sure you’re on the latest iOS version available for your device.
  • If an issue repeats, note what you did right before it happened.

Contact support: julaenw@gmail.com

When you email, include:
• Your device model (e.g., iPhone 13)
• iOS version
• What happened + steps to reproduce (if you can)
