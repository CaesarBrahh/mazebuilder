Maze Bound — Privacy Policy

Your privacy is respected. No data collection.

Maze Bound does not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, tracking SDKs, or external services of any kind.

Data Storage

All gameplay occurs locally on your device. No data is transmitted or stored externally.
